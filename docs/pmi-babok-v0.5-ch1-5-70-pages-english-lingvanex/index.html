Bilingual terminology map

TermStudy explanation
Business Analysis
Phân tích kinh doanh		Work performed to identify needs, recommend solutions, and support value delivery.
Business Need
Nhu cầu kinh doanh		A problem or opportunity important enough for an organization to consider investment.
Product Requirement
Yêu cầu sản phẩm		A condition or capability a product must have to satisfy a need.
Product Information
Thông tin sản phẩm		The collection of requirements, models, decisions, assumptions, trace links, and related information.
Solution
Giải pháp		The outcome implemented to satisfy a need; it may be a product, service, process, or organizational change.
Stakeholder
Bên liên quan		An individual, group, or organization that influences or is influenced by the outcome.
Current State
Trạng thái hiện tại		How the organization operates before the change.
Future State
Trạng thái tương lai		The desired condition after the change, connected to goals and capabilities.
Product Roadmap
Lộ trình sản phẩm		The planned sequence of product development based on value, timing, dependencies, and assumptions.
Business Case
Kế hoạch kinh doanh		The investment rationale that presents benefits, costs, risks, and recommendation.
Stakeholder Register
Sổ đăng ký stakeholder		A structured record of stakeholders, their roles, interests, influence, and engagement needs.
Transition Plan
Kế hoạch chuyển đổi		The plan that helps users, process owners, data owners, and operations move into the future state.
