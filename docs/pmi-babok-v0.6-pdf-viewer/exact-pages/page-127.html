4.3.3 DETERMINE FUTURE STATE: OUTPUTS
4.3.3.1 BUSINESS GOALS AND OBJECTIVES
Business goals and objectives identify what the business expects the portfolio, program, or project to deliver.
Business goals and objectives align to the organizational goals and objectives, but are at a lower level because they
specify stated targets that the business is seeking to achieve.
4.3.3.2 REQUIRED CAPABILITIES AND FEATURES
The required capabilities and features identify the list of net changes the organization needs to obtain in order to
achieve the desired future state. The capabilities and features listed do not prescribe a solution. Additional analysis is
still needed to determine how these capabilities and features will be delivered.
