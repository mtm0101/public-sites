Obtaining solution acceptance for a release provides the stakeholders who are accountable for the product with an
opportunity to decide whether it should be released in whole, in part, or not at all.
For solution acceptance, the term release may refer to releasing a solution or a segment of a solution into a
production environment while its development team is still responsible for it. It can also refer to releasing a solution
or a segment of it to the operational area that will take responsibility for it.
