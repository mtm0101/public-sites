7.9.5 ASSESS PRODUCT DESIGN OPTIONS: COLLABORATION POINT
Many product team roles assist with the assessment of design options. Architects provide expertise and
recommendations and are engaged to highlight complexities and risks with the design options under discussion.
Design teams contribute design ideas, sizing estimates, and any risks associated with different design options.
A project sponsor provides decision authority about funding a design based on whether it sufﬁciently addresses the
business needs and known risks; DevOps might participate by contributing infrastructure or operational considerations
and risks.
