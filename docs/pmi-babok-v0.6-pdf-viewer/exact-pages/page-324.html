9.1 EVALUATE SOLUTION PERFORMANCE
Evaluate Solution Performance is the process of evaluating a solution to determine whether the implemented
solution or solution component is delivering the business value as intended. The key beneﬁt of this process is that the
analysis provides tangible data to determine whether the solution that the business has invested in is achieving the
expected business results and serves as an input to decisions about future initiatives. The inputs, tools and techniques,
and outputs for this process are shown in Figure 9-2. Figure 9-3 depicts the data ﬂow diagram for the process.

Evaluating solution performance involves determining whether a solution or solution component that has been put
into operation is delivering the desired business value. The actual business value of a solution is measured in terms of
the business goals or objectives. Business analysis also assesses the underlying reasons behind the results obtained.
