KEY CONCEPTS FOR STAKEHOLDER ENGAGEMENT
Stakeholder Engagement involves the activities performed to analyze the needs and characteristics of stakeholders
to understand how best to identify, involve, communicate, and work with them in a collaborative manner. Stakeholder
Engagement is not unique to business analysis, but when performed within the discipline, the objective is to ensure
the best engagement with stakeholders across the business analysis processes.
