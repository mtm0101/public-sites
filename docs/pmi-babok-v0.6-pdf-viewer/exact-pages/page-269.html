7.5.5 VERIFY REQUIREMENTS: COLLABORATION POINT
On adaptive projects, all members of the product team can participate in reviewing user stories during backlog
reﬁnement and sprint planning. On predictive life cycle projects, business analysts might engage quality control team
members to perform veriﬁcation activities or other business analysts who did not create the materials.
