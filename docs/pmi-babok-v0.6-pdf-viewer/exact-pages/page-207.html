6.2.3 PREPARE FOR ELICITATION: OUTPUTS
6.2.3.1 ELICITATION PREPARATION MATERIALS
Elicitation preparation materials are items created to maximize the probability of meeting elicitation activity
objectives, while optimizing the time spent with elicitation participants. Elicitation preparation materials may be
formal or informal, depending on the preference of the facilitator. Preparation materials may include:

Elicitation activity objectives,
 An agenda,

Background information,

Questions to be discussed,

Ground rules and/or instructions to support an elicitation technique, and

Presentation materials and/or product information, including models, to help structure the elicitation activity.

6.2.5 PREPARE FOR ELICITATION: COLLABORATION POINT
Stakeholders may be consulted in advance so they are aware of what is expected of them and can be alerted if
there are preparation activities they are required to complete in advance of the elicitation. Sufﬁcient communication
is provided beforehand so that stakeholders can secure time on their calendars.
