7.2.2.9 GOAL MODEL AND BUSINESS OBJECTIVES MODEL
A goal model and business objectives model are scope models that organize and reﬂect the goals and business
objectives in relation to other product information. Goal models typically show the stakeholder goals for a solution, with
any supporting or conﬂicting goal relationships indicated. Business objectives models relate the business problems,
business objectives, and top-level features. Goal models and business objectives models are typically created at the
start of a new program or project or earlier to help prioritize programs and projects within a portfolio. They can be
created at any point, for prioritization purposes.
