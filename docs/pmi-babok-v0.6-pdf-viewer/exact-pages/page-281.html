7.7.5 PRIORITIZE REQUIREMENTS AND OTHER PRODUCT INFORMATION: COLLABORATION POINT
Product team members contribute to prioritization efforts by providing the size and risk estimates for building the
solution to the speciﬁed requirements. Product owners and product managers are typically two of the most inﬂuential
roles in establishing priorities for the portfolio, program, or project. Product sponsors can set and approve priorities,
too, and may be consulted when prioritization efforts do not result in consensus among the team.
7.8 IDENTIFY AND ANALYZE PRODUCT RISKS
Identify and Analyze Product Risks is the process of uncovering and examining assumptions and uncertainties that
could positively or negatively affect success in the deﬁnition, development, and the expected results of the solution.
