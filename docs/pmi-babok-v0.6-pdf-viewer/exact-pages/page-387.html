6.3 MANAGE CHANGES TO REQUIREMENTS AND OTHER PRODUCT INFORMATION
Manage Changes to Requirements and Other Product Information is the process of examining changes or defects
that arise during a project by understanding the value and impact of the changes. As changes are agreed upon,
information about those changes is reﬂected wherever necessary to support prioritization and eventual product
development. The key beneﬁts of this process include facilitating the incorporation of important solution changes for
projects, limiting unnecessary changes, and providing understanding of how changes will impact the end product.
