7.3.5 DEFINE AND ELABORATE REQUIREMENTS: COLLABORATION POINT
Portfolio, program, or project managers might participate in discovering business requirements and typically
own deﬁning project requirements. Business analysts are responsible for eliciting and documenting the business
requirements with other stakeholders and may be asked to support the project manager in eliciting and documenting
project requirements.
