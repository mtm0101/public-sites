Figure 4-16. Determine Viable Options and Provide Recommendation: Data Flow Diagram
Determining viable options entails conducting discussions with stakeholders and product team members and
performing further analysis to deﬁne a list of possible solution recommendations to address the business need. The
product team considers the information from the current and future state analysis when formulating these options.
The product team also determines the solution approach for each option. The solution approach is a high-level
deﬁnition about the considerations and steps necessary to deliver the solution and thereby transition the business
from the current to the future state.
