5.7.5 ASSESS BUSINESS ANALYSIS PERFORMANCE: COLLABORATION POINT
For work efforts conducted using an adaptive life cycle, project team members share responsibility for inspecting
and adapting all their practices, including those used for business analysis. For work efforts conducted using a
predictive life cycle, team members who hold other roles may provide insights into how well the analysis deliverables
created by the business analyst support their efforts or how the business analysis processes as a whole meet their
needs. One of the key roles providing these insights is a quality control analyst.
