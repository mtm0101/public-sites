Bilingual terminology map

TermStudy explanation
Elicitation
Sự khơi gợi		Planned discovery and confirmation of stakeholder knowledge, business rules, data needs, and user expectations.
Elicitation Approach
Phương pháp thu thập thông tin		The planned method for choosing techniques, participants, sequence, and confirmation path.
Unconfirmed Elicitation Results
Kết quả thu thập thông tin chưa xác nhận		Captured information that still requires review before being treated as reliable.
Confirmed Elicitation Results
Kết quả thu thập thông tin đã xác nhận		Information reviewed and accepted as a valid input for analysis.
Analysis Model
Mô hình phân tích		A structured representation that reveals relationships, data, processes, decisions, goals, states, or interactions.
Acceptance Criteria
Tiêu chí chấp nhận		Observable conditions used to determine whether a requirement or story is satisfied.
Verified Requirement
Yêu cầu đã xác minh		A requirement that meets quality standards such as clarity, consistency, feasibility, and testability.
Validated Requirement
Yêu cầu đã xác thực		A requirement confirmed to support business goals and stakeholder value.
Product Risk
Rủi ro sản phẩm		Uncertainty that may prevent the product from delivering value, being accepted, or operating successfully.
Traceability Matrix
Ma trận truy xuất nguồn gốc		A structure used to connect needs, requirements, design, tests, changes, and outcomes.
Change Decision
Quyết định thay đổi		The documented decision to accept, reject, defer, or further analyze a proposed product-information change.
Solution Evaluation Approach
Phương pháp đánh giá giải pháp		The planned way to measure whether a solution delivers intended outcomes.
Business Value Assessment
Đánh giá giá trị kinh doanh		An assessment of whether the solution has produced expected value relative to investment intent.
Release Decision
Quyết định phát hành		The go/no-go or conditional decision about whether to release a solution or increment.
