Bilingual terminology map (Bản đồ thuật ngữ song ngữ)

TermStudy explanation
Business Analysis
Phân tích kinh doanh		The practice of understanding needs, shaping product information, and helping the organization make decisions that lead to value.
Tool
Công cụ		A supporting means such as a repository, template, model, diagram, application, or structured artifact.
Technique
Kỹ thuật		A repeatable way of thinking or working, such as elicitation, modeling, prioritization, or root cause analysis.
Competency
Năng lực		A capability a BA develops to perform work effectively, including analysis, communication, leadership, and tool knowledge.
Expert Judgment
Phán quyết chuyên gia		Experience-based interpretation that should be combined with evidence, not used as unquestionable authority.
Glossary
Bảng thuật ngữ		A controlled vocabulary that keeps terms consistent across stakeholders and work products.
Index
Chỉ mục		A navigation layer that helps practitioners locate topics quickly and connect related concepts.
