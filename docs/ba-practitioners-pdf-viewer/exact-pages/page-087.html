The business analysis work plan may be included within the business analysis plan along with the other planning
decisions or organizational preferences, and the selected project life cycle can inﬂuence the approach used to
document and communicate the work plan. See Table 3-1 for a sample business analysis work plan.
Collaboration Point—It is beneﬁcial for the project manager and business analyst to collaborate on
how to document and communicate the work plan to ensure that the business analysis activities and
schedule properly integrate into the project management plan. When the business analysis process is
highly complex, the project manager may prefer that the low-level details regarding the business analysis
activities be tracked by the business analyst in a separate business analysis work plan.
