who or what will achieve it.)
Outcomes should be aligned with the organization’s mission, vision, and
strategies. (Sometimes the “R” stands for Realistic or Reasonable.)
A specific time period should be attached to the outcome, in line with the
business need. (Sometimes referred to as Timely.)
