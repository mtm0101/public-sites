This static site is a non-substitutive study companion. It summarizes, explains, and teaches the selected scope in English while keeping major titles and terms bilingual. It does not reproduce the source book as a full replacement.

Table of contents (Mục lục)

  1. Chapter 4A - Requirements Elicitation Foundations and Techniques (Chương 4A - Nền tảng và kỹ thuật khai thác yêu cầu)

    This page covers the first half of Chapter 4: how to plan, prepare, run, close, and troubleshoot elicitation. It treats elicitation as active discovery rather than passive collection, with emphasis on stakeholder readiness, questioning, listening, follow-up, and choosing techniques that fit the context.

  2. Chapter 4B - Analysis, Models, Documentation, and Agreement (Chương 4B - Phân tích, mô hình, tài liệu hóa và đồng thuận)

    This page follows the second half of Chapter 4. After information is elicited, the work shifts to analysis: refining raw information, selecting models, documenting solution requirements, prioritizing, validating, verifying, obtaining approval, and resolving conflicts.

  3. Chapter 5 - Traceability and Monitoring (Chương 5 - Truy vết và giám sát)

    Chapter 5 explains how requirements stay connected after they are written. Traceability links business needs, requirements, solution components, tests, decisions, defects, and changes so that teams can monitor scope, manage baselines, and make informed impact decisions.

  4. Chapter 6 - Solution Evaluation (Chương 6 - Đánh giá giải pháp)

    Chapter 6 focuses on whether the solution delivered the expected outcomes. It connects requirements, acceptance criteria, testing evidence, operational metrics, benefits, go/no-go decisions, signoff, and long-term performance improvement.

  5. Appendices and Glossary Reference (Phụ lục và thuật ngữ tham chiếu)

    The remaining back matter is handled as a study reference rather than a reproduced copy. This page explains how to use the contributors/reviewers appendix, bibliography, and glossary responsibly when studying Chapters 4-6.
