Bilingual terminology map

TermStudy meaning
Business Analysis
Phân tích kinh doanh		A discipline for understanding business needs and recommending solutions that deliver value.
Business Need
Nhu cầu kinh doanh		A problem or opportunity that justifies analysis and possible investment.
Product Information
Thông tin sản phẩm		Requirements, models, decisions, assumptions, trace links, and other information about the product.
Business Case
Kế hoạch kinh doanh		The investment rationale that explains benefits, costs, risks, options, and product scope.
Stakeholder Register
Sổ đăng ký stakeholder		A record of people or groups who affect or are affected by the initiative.
Traceability
Truy xuất nguồn gốc		The ability to connect goals, requirements, designs, tests, changes, and releases.
Acceptance Criteria
Tiêu chí chấp nhận		Conditions that demonstrate a requirement has been met.
Release Decision
Quyết định phát hành		The decision to release based on requirements, acceptance evidence, risks, and readiness.
