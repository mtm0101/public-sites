P2.5.1 Prepare for Transition to Future State (Chuẩn bị chuyển đổi sang trạng thái tương lai)

What it is

Transition preparation assesses readiness and plans how people, process, data, systems, support, and communication will move toward the desired future state. It gives the BA a structured way to reduce ambiguity and make product information usable by the wider team.

Why it is used

For P2-5-1, the value is to keep business goals, stakeholder intent, product scope, and delivery decisions aligned. Without it, teams may deliver quickly while still solving the wrong problem.

When to use it

Use it before a release that changes user behavior, operations, training, migration, or support routines. Use P2-5-1 with extra care when the next decision may affect investment, scope, priority, design, testing, transition, or release confidence.

How to apply it

For P2-5-1, begin with the decision to support, inspect the available inputs, choose a technique that fits the uncertainty, create a reviewable output, and connect the result to the next BA or delivery activity.
